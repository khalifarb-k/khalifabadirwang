Welcome To Kayo Stars Ladies Football Club

The team was established to curb the gap in the community when it comes to women soccer, to build these young women to be physically, mentally, psychologically and emotionally strong on and off the field. To foster the development of a positive mental attitude and good sportsmanship. It was the founder's desire to empower these young women so that they become productive participants in Potchefstroom long term economic and social development, to promote physical fitness with a desire to challenge these young women and help them to stay out of the street and strive to provide our youth with an opportunity to learn, have fun and experience the importance of team work.

ABOUT US

A Team Of Young Talented Ladies.

The team is from Ikageng Location in Potchefstroom in the North West Province. Kayo Stars Ladies Football Club is Affiliated under JB Marks Local Football Association (JB Marks LFA) Registered under South African Football Association (SAFA) Officially participating in DR Kenneth Kaunda Regional Women's Soccer League
